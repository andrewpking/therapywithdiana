Adult, Individual Treatment

I offer an extensive range of services for adult (18+) individuals. Some areas that I specialize in include depression, anxiety, trauma, life adjustments, grief, and transitions. My interventions are individualized and tailored to your needs.

Why Online

Online Therapy is convenient for your lifestyle needs. It is competely confidential using our HIPAA compliant telehealth service. This services is easily accessed by anyone who has a computer and a reliable internet connection. Due to the nature of online therapy, your sessions grant you anonymity that was not available before.

Free Phone Consultation

Not surprisingly, having a good fit in the client-therapist relationship plays a critical role in the success of addressing your issues. My intention is to help you feel unconditional acceptance and compassion in our relationship. My style is friendly and light hearted, I also use humor quite a bit. I look forward to discuss with you the issues that bring you to seek help.

Intake

During our first 90 minute session together, I will ask you about current concerns, as well as, past medical and psychiatric history. An assessment allows us to efficiently and effectively evaluate current symptoms so that we may identify a treatment plan to best meet your needs.

Mental Health Emergencies

A mental health crisis is any situation in which a person's actions, feelings, and behaviors can lead to them hurting themselves or others, and/or put them at risk of being unable to care for themselves or function in the community in a healthy manner. If you are experiencing an emergency call the mental health crisis hotline at 1-800-273-8255, 988 or go to your nearest emergency department.
